About our team

We are a small team of four students working for the University of California, San Diego Synposium hosted by 7th College. Built over a few weeks in Winter of 2023, this project is intended to clearly and simply present data on local and national environmental issues, news, volunteer opportunities, and more. By way of our interactive map, we hope to increase the availability and ease of use for students like us to connect with their local communities and find ways to contribute to helping our planet. We hope to become a reputable source for college students to learn more about the climate crisis while simultaneously giving them avenues to volunteer by connecting them to local environmental organizations and projects.
